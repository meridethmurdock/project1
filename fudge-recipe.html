Sis Ennis Long’s Never Fail Fudge

"Mother made this every Christmas with her two sisters, Helen and Inez. Couldn’t wait to dive into it!"

    Prep Time: 45 mins
    Cool Time: 1 - 2

  • 1/2 lb butter
  • 4 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 3 6oz pkgs semi-sweet chocolate
  • 8 oz jar of marshmallow cream
  • 3-4 cups chopped pecans (optional)

Step 1

Place butter, sugar, and milk in heavy sauce pan and bring to boil, stirring constantly. Boil for 4 minutes, stirring.



Step 2

Add chocolate, marshmallow cream, and pecans. Stir until chocolate and marshmallow cream is dissolved. Pour into buttered pans.



Step 3 - Enjoy!

Let cool in fridge, and enjoy! Send Meme a picture when you're done!
