Sharon Pepper lives in Huntsville, Alabama with her husband, Steve (Pawpaw), dog, Luke, and fiesty cat, Aubrie. She loves cooking for her best friends, Mary and Virginia, her copius church friends, and, of course, her family!

Last year, my grandma gave all of her grandchildren a recipe book filled with the recipes to some of the best food I've ever tasted. It was such an amazing and thoughtful surprise, so I wanted to surprise her this Christmas with her very own website!
