Chicken and Dressing

"This chicken and dressing is a must-have at Thanksgiving!"

    Prep Time: 45 mins
    Cook Time: 45 mins

  • 1 9” cake of cornbread, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 whole chicken, cooked and drained of broth, chopped
  • 2-4 cups of broth from chicken (as needed)
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 small onion
  • 1/2 cup celery, chopped
  • 1-2 tbsp sage
  • 4 slices of bread

Step 1

Preheat oven to 400F. In a small saucepan, cook butter, onion, and celery for about 30 minutes on med-low heat.



Step 2

Crumble cornbread and loaf bread together. Pour chicken broth over breads (2 cups or more) Hands come in handy! If it’s too dry add more chicken broth! Add sage. Mix well.



Step 3

Put in 9x12 casserole dish. Lay chopped chicken on top and bake for 45 minutes to an hour. Stir 2 / 3 times while cooking. You can also add some of the chicken into the dressing.



Step 4 - Enjoy!
