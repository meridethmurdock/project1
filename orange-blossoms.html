Orange Blossoms

"Whitney and Meridethâ€™s favorite! These fluffy mini-cupcakes dipped into an orange glaze melt in your mouth."

    Prep Time: 45 mins
    Cool Time: 1 - 2 hours

    Cake:

  • 1 pkg yellow cake mix
  • 1 3.4 oz pkg instant lemon pudding mix
  • 4 eggs
  • 3/4 cup cold water
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil

    • Glaze:

  • 3 cups confectioners sugar
  • 1/2 cup frozen orange concentrate thawed
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 3 tbsp water

Step 1

Preheat oven to 325F. Grease two miniature muffin pans.



Step 2

In a large mixing bowl, combine the cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, water, and oil. Beat well.



Step 3

Fill the prepared muffin pans, using about 1 tbsp of batter for each cupcake. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cakes spring back when touched.



Step 4

For the glaze, mix the confectioners sugar, orange juice, butter, and water until smooth.



Step 5

Dip the blossoms in the glaze immediately after removing from the oven. Drain on waxed paper.



Step 6 - Enjoy!

Let cool (or don't! They're even better warm!), and enjoy! Send Meme a picture when you're done!
